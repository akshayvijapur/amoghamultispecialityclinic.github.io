About Us

Amogha Multispeciality Hospital

Welcome to Amogha Multispeciality Hospital, where your health and well-being are our foremost priorities. Founded by Dr. Avadusiddha Arakeri M.D(General Medicine) an accomplished specialist in General Medicine and Santosh Arakeri M.D(OBG) an accomplished specialist in Obstetrics & Gynecology, our hospital is a testament to a deep-rooted commitment to quality healthcare.

At Amogha, we have built a state-of-the-art facility that integrates advanced medical technology with a patient-centric approach. Our dedicated team of medical professionals, including specialists in General Medicine, Diabetes Care, and Obstetrics & Gynecology, works collaboratively to provide a wide range of healthcare services.

We believe in comprehensive, personalized care, ensuring that every patient receives the attention and support they deserve. Whether you're seeking routine check-ups, managing a chronic condition, or embarking on the journey of parenthood, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

Our mission is to offer expert medical care that emphasizes prevention, early intervention, and a holistic approach to health. We understand that good health is a foundation for a fulfilling life, and we're here to support you on your path to better health.

Amogha Multispeciality Hospital is not just a healthcare provider; we're your trusted partner in health. We are deeply embedded in the local community, and we take pride in the trust that our patients place in us.

Thank you for choosing Amogha Multispeciality Hospital. Your health is our mission, and we're here to serve you with compassion, expertise, and a dedication to excellence.

Our Solution

Heart care
